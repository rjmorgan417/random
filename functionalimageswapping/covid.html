COVID Me

As I see it, there are several positive things that have come out of the pandemic for the Morgans.

First, I think that I made strides to complete the transformation from a career Soldier to a private civilian. Isolation forced me to become more self-reliant as a civilian.

Second, the pandemic allowed me to deepen my connection with my wife and daughters, and allowed me to be very active in their educations.

Third, I relearned that barbers are only as important as you make them.

Fourth, Diet Dr. Pepper and Dunkin' Donuts Caramel Coffee are some of the background secret keys to happiness.

Bottomline: We're gonna get through this; this too shall pass.
