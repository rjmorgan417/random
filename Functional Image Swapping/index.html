About Me

My wife Monica and I live in Battlefield, Missouri with our two daughters, Makenna and Madeline. Both Monica and I grew up in Springfield, graduating from Kickapoo High School in 1990 and 1991, respectively.

I retired from the Army in November of 2018 following 23 years of active service. In those years, we (the family) lived in multiple US states, and I (with the Army) visited lots of cool places, and completed overseas tours in Iraq, Afghanistan, and South Korea. The last job I held in the Army was the commander of the 2nd Squadron, 11th Armored Cavalry Regiment out of Fort Irwin, California.

I'm currently pursuing an Associate's Degree in Computer Information Science, and if all goes as planned, I will complete the program in the spring.

As far as icecream goes, I am a chocolate chip cookie dough fan. However, my favorite Andy's frozen custard flavor is Key Lime Pie. I mean, I do have to keep my imaginary subscribers happy.
